Course information

For official course information see Sakai (syllabus, calendar, paper PDFs, etc). This course makes extensive use of R and assumes basic familiarity with base R (not packages) as a prerequisite. A self-quiz is available here, with answers provided here. You can also find a list of base R functions that one should be familiar with.
